Action Items

1 Automate Payment Process High Priority
Owner: Tech/Product Team
Description:

Automate the reward payment system so that when a client qualifies based on criteria, payment is triggered automatically without manual intervention.

Current Issue:

Manual payment processing causing delays and customer dissatisfaction.

Goal:

Payments should be automatic when client qualifies - client qualification should be the trigger for payment.

2 Implement Payment Forecasting
Owner: Finance/Operations Team
Description:

Create a forecast system to predict reward payment amounts and pre-fund accounts.

Example:

If 10,000 users with 20% conversion at $X per user, need to have funds available upfront (e.g., $2,000 ready for distribution).

Purpose:

Enable automated payment distribution by ensuring funds are available when needed.

3 Design 6-Step Gamification Strategy
Owner: Marketing Team
Description:

Implement the six-step customer journey with gamified bonuses:

  • Step 1: First week onboarding journey with 5 gamified bonuses
  • Step 2: Viral referral program leveraging trust networks
  • Step 3: User-generated content and authentic testimonials
  • Step 4: Financial education content
  • Step 5: Rewards/loyalty program ("Más Bambu")
  • Step 6: To be determined
Goal:

Keep customers engaged beyond 2-3 months and improve retention.

4 Create First Week Bonus Structure
Owner: Marketing/Product Team
Description:

Implement progressive bonus system for first 7 days:

  • Day 1: Fund account with $10+ → receive $3 bonus
  • Day 3: Activate card → receive $5 bonus
  • Day 5: Set up direct deposit → receive $25 bonus
  • Day 7: Complete all tasks → receive $10 bonus
Total Potential:

~$43 in bonuses during first week

Note:

Day 5 direct deposit bonus may need adjustment - need to verify if we can track when clients receive their first paycheck with us.

5 Address Funding Methods Strategy
Owner: Product/Marketing Team
Description:

Re-evaluate promotion strategy for funding methods.

Current Situation:
  • Heavy focus on direct deposit and cash methods
  • Venmo, Zelle, and other P2P methods being deprioritized
  • However, P2P methods are growing and may be easier for some user segments
Question to Answer:

Should we also promote alternative funding methods (Venmo, Zelle) despite being less formal?

6 Develop Financial Education Rewards Program
Owner: Marketing/Content Team
Description:

Create educational content about how to use the app and financial literacy.

Reward Structure:

Clients who complete financial education courses receive bonuses.

Why:

Survey data shows many users "don't know how to use it".

Benefit:

Educated clients = better long-term retention and engagement.

7 Investigate Direct Deposit Awareness
Owner: UX/Marketing Research Team
Description:

Research if gig economy workers (Uber, DoorDash, construction, etc.) understand what "direct deposit" means.

Alternative Naming:

Consider "depósito de sueldo" or other clearer language for Spanish-speaking users.

Question:

How do gig apps (DoorDash, Uber, etc.) label this feature in their apps?

8 Explore Strategic Partnership with Gig Apps
Owner: Business Development Team
Description:

Investigate partnership opportunities with platforms like DoorDash, Uber.

Rationale:

Large portion of user base works for these apps.

User Behavior Data:
  • Average: $650-$1,100 per transaction
  • Monthly average: ~$1,700-$800 per user
  • Transaction frequency: ~3-3.5 transactions per month
  • Pattern: Weekly/bi-weekly cashouts
9 Customer Journey Optimization High Priority
Owner: Product/CX Team
Description:

Attack the full customer journey from start to finish.

Current Problems:
  • High rewards budget for new accounts
  • Clients take long time to fund accounts
  • Activity drops after 2-3 months
  • Need to reduce time to activation
Goal:

Fast activation → sustained engagement through multiple touchpoints.

10 Review and Implement Deep Advisory Recommendations
Owner: Leadership/Marketing Team
Description:

Review the Deep Advisory analysis document and implement recommendations.

Key Finding:

Activation problem, not just acquisition problem.

Next Steps:

Implement the actionable items from the strategy document (contains step-by-step daily strategy).

11 Address Customer Satisfaction Issues High Priority
Owner: CX/Product Team
Description:

Address the drop in customer satisfaction scores.

Root Cause:

Customer satisfaction has dropped primarily due to delayed reward payments, not product issues.

Connection:

This is directly tied to Action Item #1 (automate payments).

12 Create Referral Program Structure
Owner: Marketing/Growth Team
Description:

Design viral referral program with attractive incentives.

Example Structure Referenced:
  • $25 bonus for setting up direct deposit
  • $15 bonus for card purchase within 15 days
  • Total potential: $80+ per activated referral
Comparison Point:

Other platforms offer $50-$100 for deposits of $1,000-$30,000.

Data Needed:

Current average direct deposit amount (pull September data).
